Board and Training Packages

Full Service Board

Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily still cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out. All stall bedding, hay, and grain included in your board.

Partial Board

Partial board includes a 12x12 stall, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and supplements. All stall bedding, grain and hay are included in your board. With artial board you are responsible for cleaning your own stalls at least once a day. Partial boards who fail to clean their stallls will be cleaned by staff and be charged additionally.

Pasture Board

Horses on pasture boards will have free roam to 20 arces of land and have access to several run-in shelters. Grain, hay, and water is provided as part of your pasture board. Blanketing is avalible for an additional charge, as needed.
