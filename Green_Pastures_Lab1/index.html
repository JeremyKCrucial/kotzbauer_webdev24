Full Service Board

Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily stall cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay and grain is included in your board.

Partial Board

Partial care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily stall cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay and grain is included in your board. With partial care board you are responsible for cleaning your own stalls at least once a day. Partial boards who fail to keep their stalls clean will have their stalls cleaned by staff and be charged additionally.

Pasture Board

Horses on pasture board have free roam to 20 arces of pasture and have access to several run-in shelters. Grain, hay and water is provided as part of your pasture board. Blanketing is avalible for an additional charge, as needed.
